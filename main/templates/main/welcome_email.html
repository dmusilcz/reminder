Hello {{ username }}, welcome to NeverExpire! You are now ready to save your documents and choose when to be reminded of their expiration.


Please follow the instructions below so you don't miss your email reminders in case some of our emails get delivered to SPAM/Promotions/other non-primary folder where you might miss them.


For Gmail:

  1. If present, click the 'Report not spam' button
  2. Find the email in your inbox again and open it
  3. Click the three bullets button ('More') in the top of this email
  4. Select 'Filter messages like these'
  5. Click 'Create filter'
  6. Check 'Never send it to Spam'
  7. Check 'Categorize as' and select 'Primary' from the dropdown list
  8. Check 'Also apply filter to X matching conversations'
  9. Click 'Create filter'
